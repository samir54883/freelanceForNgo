Our Key Activities

Road Safety & Awareness

Organized helmet distributions and traffic drives at major intersections before state campaigns began.

Education Support

Supplied school essentials to prevent dropout among underserved children, especially girls.

Child Health Camps

Free medical checkups and medicines for children in underprivileged areas.

National Integration

Hosted events promoting peace among diverse religions and communities.

Clothing Drives

Blanket and garment distribution for the homeless during winter months.

Elder Support

Felicitation of senior citizens and health-focused programs for elder care.

Adult Health Camps

Free screenings for BP, diabetes, and general health for economically weak sections.

Condolence Meetings

Memorials held for poets, teachers, and social leaders with community participation.

Police-Public Harmony

Felicitating police officers to strengthen community respect for law enforcement.

Anti-Drug Campaigns

Awareness drives, rallies, and school programs focused on substance abuse prevention.

Youth Engagement

Organized drawing contests and sports events to develop creativity and teamwork.

Environment Action

Cleanliness drives and tree plantation in collaboration with Kolkata Police.
